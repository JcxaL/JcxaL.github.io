Apple Liquid Glass — WWDC 2026
Introduced in iOS 26 and macOS Tahoe, Liquid Glass is Apple's unified material system. It uses real-time environment sampling, optical distortion at rounded edges, and layered translucency to make UI elements look like physical glass objects sitting in front of the content beneath them. In CSS we approximate this with backdrop-filter, layered box-shadows, and animated shimmer gradients.
