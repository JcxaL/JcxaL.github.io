Glass tuning

Liquid Glass Panels

Not flat blur-behind glassmorphism — the backdrop visibly refracts near each panel's edge, with a faint color-fringe like real glass. One SVG <filter> chain, referenced by backdrop-filter: url(#id).

Settings

An edge-weighted displacement map bends the moving backdrop most near the border, least at the center — like light through a lens rim.

Notification

Drag the sliders to feel each layer of the filter chain independently: displacement strength, frost blur, and RGB channel offset.

Now Playing

The colorful blobs drifting behind these panels exist purely so the refraction has something to distort — watch them warp at the card edges.
