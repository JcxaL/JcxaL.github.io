Slide from left

Directional reveals make scanning feel natural. Left-anchored content slides in from the left; image counterparts arrive from the right — guiding the eye across the layout.

Animating only opacity and transform keeps work on the GPU compositor thread. No repaints, no layout shifts — just buttery smooth 60fps motion on any device.
