Spring Equation

F = -k·x − d·v. Stiffness k pulls toward origin, damping d bleeds velocity. When d < 2√(k·m) the system oscillates — the classic underdamped spring.

Why it Feels Right

iOS rubber-banding uses exactly this model. The overshoot and settle matches our intuition of physical objects, making digital interfaces feel touchable.

Implementation

Each frame: acceleration = (-k·displacement − d·velocity) / mass. Integrate with Euler. requestAnimationFrame keeps it GPU-paced at 60fps.
