Scroll-Driven
3D Depth Reveal

Pure CSS · animation-timeline: view() · each card tilts from its own angle

↓ scroll to reveal
🌌

Spatial Entry

Each card enters from a unique Z-depth and Y-rotation, creating the illusion of objects flying in from different angles in 3D space.

⚡

Zero JavaScript

The entire reveal sequence runs on animation-timeline: view() — no scroll listeners, no IntersectionObserver, no JS at all.

🎯

Per-Element Range

Using animation-range: entry 0% entry 40% each card's animation completes in the first 40% of its scroll entrance — snappy and satisfying.

🔮

Blur Defocus

A filter: blur() animates from 8px to 0 in sync with the 3D transform — mimicking a camera lens pulling focus as cards arrive.

💫

CSS Custom Properties

--rot-y per card sets a unique initial Y-rotation. One keyframe definition drives infinite variation across all cards.

🛸

GPU Accelerated

Only transform, opacity, and filter are animated — all composited on the GPU. Zero layout recalculation, silky at 60 fps.
