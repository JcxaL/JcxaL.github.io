Editor

Resize me by dragging either divider left or right. Widths persist to localStorage and clamp between sensible min/max bounds so no pane can be crushed to zero.
Each divider also supports double-click to reset that side back to its default split, matching the ergonomics of editor workspaces like VS Code's panel resize handles.
Try narrowing your browser — panes reflow and the min-widths keep everything usable down to small screens.
