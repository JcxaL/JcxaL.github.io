Native Scroll-Button Carousel

The ::scroll-button() and ::scroll-marker pseudo-elements let the browser generate real, focusable, auto-disabling prev/next buttons and dot markers for a scroller — with zero buttons in the HTML. Where support is missing, a JS fallback (feature-detected via @supports selector()) takes over so the carousel still works everywhere.
