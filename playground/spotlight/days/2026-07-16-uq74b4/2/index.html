CSS-Only Dock Magnifier

A macOS-style dock that magnifies whichever icon you hover and cascades a falloff to its neighbors — with zero JavaScript driving the effect. The forward falloff uses plain adjacent-sibling selectors (:hover + .item); the backward falloff wraps the same selector inside :has(), turning it into a "previous-sibling" combinator.
