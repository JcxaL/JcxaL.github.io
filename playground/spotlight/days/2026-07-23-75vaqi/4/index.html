How it works

Diagonals split each card into 4 triangles. The entry/exit edge is computed from cursor position each time, so exits retreat the way they came.

Direction-Aware Hover Cards

Hover any card from a different edge — the reveal panel always slides in and out through the edge the cursor actually crossed, computed live from two diagonal cross-products.
