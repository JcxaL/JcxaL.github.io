## Arrival

One establishing paragraph. Where the train doors open, what hits first —
light, smell, noise. Keep it short; the hero image does the wide shot.

## The Story

The main body. What happened, in order or not. Photos go here with
placard-style captions; six to twelve, each with place-specific alt text.

## Field Notes

Short observations that did not fit the story — food, small kindnesses,
things misread. Bullet points welcome.

## Know Before You Go

Practicalities in plain terms: getting in, getting around, what it cost,
what to skip. The facts plate covers numbers; this covers judgment.
